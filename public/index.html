Google Analytics, Privacy, and Event Tracking

This article was originally published on the Mozilla Hacks blog. Many of us use Google Analytics (GA) or similar third-party services to keep track of how people interact with our websites; telling us things like when people visit and what they click. This data can help us make important decisions, such as when to schedule maintenance or if a feature can be removed. Because these services are installed on multiple websites these third-parties can collect a lot of information about people as they move around the internet. Read the full article
